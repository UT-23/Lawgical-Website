# About Section Direction

## Goal

Transform the About section from a static timeline into an interactive storytelling experience.

## Requirements

- Scroll-driven progression
- Timeline animation
- Gold progress line
- Active milestone highlighting
- Subtle reveal effects
- Premium legal aesthetic

## Reference Inspiration

- Apple product storytelling
- KodyTechnolab reveal pacing
- High-end consulting firms

## Interaction Concepts

### Scroll To Reveal Our Story

Introduce the section with:

"Scroll To Reveal Our Story"

Accompanied by:

- subtle animated mouse indicator
- gentle pulse animation
- elegant fade-in

### Dynamic Timeline

As users scroll:

- Timeline line progressively fills
- Current milestone becomes active
- Past milestones move into completed state
- Future milestones remain muted

### Journey Path

Optional enhancement:

- Replace rigid vertical timeline with a flowing gold journey path
- Path draws itself as user scrolls
- Connects major milestones visually

## Design Principles

Communicate:

- trust
- authority
- progression
- credibility
- long-term expertise

Avoid:

- gimmicks
- gaming effects
- excessive motion
- distracting animations

## Objective

Create anticipation and discovery while maintaining the professionalism expected from a premium legal advisory firm.
