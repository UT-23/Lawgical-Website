# Master Redesign Vision

## North star
Transform the site from a conventional law-firm brochure website into a premium, cinematic, interactive experience that feels:
- credible
- sharp
- modern
- motion-rich
- globally competitive
- business-first, not generic

## Core design shift
The current site should be replaced with a more immersive experience built around:
- strong visual hierarchy
- layered depth
- scroll storytelling
- subtle 3D cues
- interactive cards
- magnetic CTAs
- animated transitions between sections
- moving background systems
- premium editorial layouts

## What the new site should feel like
- A legal brand with the polish of a high-end design studio
- A homepage that behaves like a cinematic landing experience
- Service pages that feel like interactive case-study modules
- Team pages that feel like a people-led authority showcase
- News pages that feel like an intelligent editorial feed
- Contact pages that feel conversion-focused and polished

## Design principles
1. Keep the same information, but change the delivery completely.
2. Every section should contain at least one interactive moment.
3. Motion should support clarity, not distract from it.
4. Use contrast, depth, and rhythm to guide attention.
5. The site should feel custom-built, not templated.
6. The brand should read as premium, exacting, and modern.
7. Avoid repeating the current layout language or spacing patterns.

## Visual direction
### Palette
- Deep neutral base
- Warm ivory surfaces
- Accent color used sparingly
- One strong highlight color for calls to action
- Optional subtle gradient overlays for atmosphere

### Typography
- Elegant serif for authority moments
- Clean sans-serif for body and UI
- Large editorial headlines
- Compact UI labels and microcopy

### Layout language
- Asymmetrical sections
- Split screens
- Full-bleed panels
- Staggered card grids
- Large image blocks with text overlays
- Sticky side notes
- Repeating section rhythm with occasional visual resets

### Signature experiences
- 3D hero composition or parallax skyline scene
- Scroll-triggered service reveal
- Interactive industry map or sector constellation
- Team cards with layered hover depth
- Case-style article cards with movement
- Conversion CTA strip with motion and emphasis
- Footer with a highly designed “closing statement” feel

## Redesign outcome
The site should feel like a premium law brand that understands modern digital storytelling.
