# Page-by-Page Redesign Blueprint

## Home
### Current job
Introduce the brand and route users toward services, people, proof, and contact.

### New approach
- Cinematic hero with layered motion
- Strong value proposition immediately visible
- Sticky scroll navigation or section jump rail
- Services presented as interactive story cards
- Industries shown as a visual grid or rotating matrix
- Team and news previews as credibility blocks
- Conversion CTA strip before the footer

### Add
- A moving background motif
- One immersive interactive element
- One “why trust us” proof module
- One compact stats band
- One conversion CTA near the fold end

## About
### New approach
- Split narrative: story on one side, visual timeline on the other
- Founder / firm philosophy module
- Principles cards with hover interaction
- Timeline or milestones with scroll activation
- Office / region visual with subtle motion

### Add
- Company story timeline
- Values cards
- Mission and approach block
- Optional “how we work” process panel

## Services
### New approach
- Services as a modular explorer instead of a simple list
- Filterable categories or toggles
- Expanded service panels with layered detail
- Sticky summary panel for selected service
- CTA after each service to request a consultation

### Add
- Service comparison mode
- Animated iconography
- Outcome-focused proof points
- Side note with typical client scenarios

## Industries
### New approach
- Industry cards with hover reveals and data-style presentation
- More visual authority for each sector
- Optional constellation or grid map layout
- Stronger “we understand your world” message

### Add
- Sector outcome highlights
- Use-case chips
- Interactive highlight on hover or selection
- Scroll-triggered sector sequencing

## Team
### New approach
- Premium portrait wall with depth and spacing
- Hover reveals credentials, expertise, and contact actions
- Sort / filter by expertise if helpful
- Add editorial labels for seniority and practice areas

### Add
- Quick bio preview overlay
- LinkedIn / email action row
- “Meet the team” storytelling intro
- Better portrait presentation and crop control

## News
### New approach
- Feature article with strong editorial presence
- Masonry or magazine-style feed
- Clear category chips and reading-time cues
- Motion on hover and route transitions
- Better scanning experience for thought leadership

### Add
- Featured story hero
- Horizontal or card-based topic filter
- Pull-quote or insight rail
- More premium article card hierarchy

## Contact
### New approach
- Strong conversion layout with reassurance
- Split form and contact information
- Light map or location scene
- Instant feedback on form interactions
- Confidence-building copy and availability cues

### Add
- Sticky contact details panel
- Clear response-time expectations
- Service selection in the form
- Confirmation state animation

## Footer
### New approach
- Make the footer feel like a final brand statement
- Add depth, hierarchy, and utility
- Include navigation, contact, location, and a concise closing message
- Use a premium multi-column layout with motion on reveal
