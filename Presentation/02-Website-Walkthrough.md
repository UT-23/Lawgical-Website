# Website Walkthrough
## Page-by-Page Editorial Guide

This document walks through each route on the rebuilt website, explaining its purpose, target audience, content structure, interactive details, and commercial value.

---

## 1. Homepage (`/`)
* **Purpose:** Introduce the brand positioning of Lawgical Group immediately through a cinematic entrance sequence and coordinate grid.
* **Audience:** Global corporate general counsels, investment bankers, developers, and compliance leads looking for GCC legal representation.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Hero Section:** Displays the main value proposition: *"Law That Moves At The Speed Of Business"* over the rotating Legal Intelligence grid.
  * **Firm Positioning:** Outlines the boutique approach combined with global calibre, showcasing the four core values (Integrity, Client Focus, Precision, Innovation).
  * **Expertise Ecosystem:** High-level summary of the six core practice areas.
  * **Trust Constellation:** Organic mosaic displaying all 25 client logos.
  * **Insights Hub:** Grid showing the lead featured article and recent briefings.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Arrival Loader:** Scales and reveals the official logo icon followed by a smooth text wordmark fade-in.
  * **Spotlight Tracker:** Moves a subtle radial highlight on the background grid as the mouse moves.
  * **Compass Cursor:** Custom pointer tracking that shifts to appropriate states (arrows, play symbols, view indicators) dynamically.
* **Business Value:** Establishes first-impression authority. It immediately sets the firm apart from traditional, template-based websites and drives visitors to explore deeper services.

---

## 2. About (`/about`)
* **Purpose:** Detail the heritage, boutique narrative, and foundational values behind the firm's GCC operations.
* **Audience:** Long-term partners, institutional investors, and regional boards requiring an overview of the firm's background.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Our Story Narrative:** Details the history of the Dubai-headquartered boutique.
  * **Values Module:** Features detailed descriptions of the firm's core operational values.
  * **Meet the Team CTA:** Invites users to view the partner profiles.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Reveal Transitions:** Smooth paragraph reveals as the user scrolls.
  * **Hover Accents:** Interactive cards featuring vector logo geometry icons that glow on mouseover.
* **Business Value:** Contextualizes the firm’s scale and operational ethics. It builds human trust by demonstrating regional longevity.

---

## 3. Expertise (`/expertise`)
* **Purpose:** Present the detailed capabilities of the firm's six key practice areas.
* **Audience:** In-house counsel, operations managers, and commercial entities facing litigation, arbitration, or regulatory compliance mandates.
* **Key Content:**
  * Interactive capabilities list covering: Corporate Law, Arbitration, Litigation, Debt Collection, Regulatory & Compliance, and Strategic Advisory.
  * Deep-dive checklists highlighting transactional scope (e.g., M&A, DIAC/ICC arbitration, DIFC court litigation, AML compliance checks).
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Interactive Navigators:** Anchored transitions that smooth-scroll to specific practice details.
  * **Chevron Details:** Reveal points with micro-animations.
* **Business Value:** Clearly maps practice scope to user needs, proving technical capability and converting specific legal inquiries directly into the contact funnel.

---

## 4. Industries (`/industries`)
* **Purpose:** Outline the 12 key sector verticals in which the firm regularly advises.
* **Audience:** Real estate developers, construction executives, financial technology founders, energy operators, and logistics managers.
* **Key Content:**
  * Grid of key industries: Financial Services, Real Estate, Construction, Energy, Technology, Healthcare, Retail & FMCG, Hospitality, Logistics, Manufacturing, Media, and Family Offices.
  * Detailed descriptions of sector advisory scopes.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Parallax Marquee Ribbons:** Three rows of cards sliding at different speeds. Hovering pauses the marquee and highlights the industry.
  * **Touch Swiping:** Swipes naturally on mobile screens.
* **Business Value:** Proves commercial familiarity. Multi-national corporations trust firms that demonstrate deep familiarity with their specific industries.

---

## 5. People (`/people`)
* **Purpose:** Introduce the 11 legal advocates and operations leads behind the practice.
* **Audience:** Clients requiring biographical background on their assigned legal counsel.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Dual Leadership Spotlight:** Dedicated large-format showcases for Managing Partner Kishore Mulani and UAE Advocate Dr. Essa Al Nuaimi.
  * **Partners & Operations Grid:** Spotlights Fathima Salam (Head of Operations), Asaf Rizvi (Corporate Partner), and Sara Essam (Dispute Resolution Partner).
  * **Associates & Counsel Row:** Details senior associates and associates.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **3D Depth Hover:** Tilt transforms on cards as the pointer moves.
  * **View Bio slide-ups:** Interactive overlays displaying detailed bios inside the card bounds.
  * **Manually Optimized Crop Framing:** Focuses on facial coordinates (top-third alignment) across all viewports.
* **Business Value:** Essential for local courts right of audience credibility. Spotlighting Dr. Essa Al Nuaimi alongside Kishore Mulani proves both court litigation and corporate transactional depth.

---

## 6. Clients (`/clients`)
* **Purpose:** Highlight client relationships, testimonials, and video-based reviews.
* **Audience:** Prospective corporate clients seeking social proof and third-party validation.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Collection Reel:** A cinematic video reel summary of client work.
  * **Testimonials Video Grid:** 4 client testimonials (Jalpa, Mostafa, Shadhad, Srinivas Singh) detailing specific cases.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Video Modal Overlay:** Clicking a card pauses ambient scrolling, opens a fullscreen cinema player, and auto-focuses the video.
  * **Hover Video Previews:** Gently loops muted previews of the testimonials on pointer hover.
* **Business Value:** Social proof is the highest driver of conversion. Real clients detailing dispute wins or compliance resolutions provides credible trust signals.

---

## 7. CSR (`/csr`)
* **Purpose:** Showcase pro-bono campaigns, environmental initiatives, and social wellness programs.
* **Audience:** Non-governmental organizations, local communities, and ESG-conscious corporate groups.
* **Key Content:**
  * **CSR Video Hub:** Masonry video grid displaying five awareness campaigns (Breast Cancer, Education, Stress Awareness, UN Social Advocacy, Gratitude).
  * **ESG Commitments:** Details environmental and wellness policies.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Masonry Video Playback:** Hover-sensitive video summaries with smooth scale popups.
* **Business Value:** Establishes corporate citizenship. Premium global brands prefer to partner with law firms that actively give back and follow ESG criteria.

---

## 8. Legal Location Atlas (`/lla`)
* **Purpose:** Detail the firm's regional GCC jurisdictional reach and affiliate desks.
* **Audience:** Multinational entities operating cross-border across Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and the UAE.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Interactive SVG GCC Map:** Displays nodes for Dubai, Riyadh, Doha, Muscat, Kuwait, Manama, and Cairo.
  * **Jurisdiction Lists:** Outlines operational scenarios and local agency codes.
  * **Dubai HQ Details:** Contains direct Google Maps integration and coordinates.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **SVG Map Node Highlighting:** Hovering a map region expands coordinate indicators and updates the details panel instantly.
* **Business Value:** Demonstrates cross-border capability. General counsels can visually confirm that Lawgical handles filings and litigation outside of Dubai.

---

## 9. Insights (`/insights`)
* **Purpose:** Serve as the firm's legal intelligence hub, housing market analysis and regulatory briefings.
* **Audience:** General counsels and board members seeking updates on regional legal reforms.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Featured Intelligence Card:** Large-format cover story focusing on primary reforms (e.g., UAE Arbitration Reforms).
  * **Briefings & Analysis:** Horizontal list of secondary briefings.
  * **Archived Reports:** Typography-focused sidebar grid of older legal notes.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Read indicators:** Rebuilt cursor expands to display the "READ" state.
  * **Category Filtering Chips:** Instant dynamic state filters to narrow down briefings.
* **Business Value:** Positions the firm as active thought leaders. Clients trust advocates who demonstrate clear, up-to-date knowledge of regional amendments.

---

## 10. Contact (`/contact`)
* **Purpose:** Capture targeted, high-value inquiries.
* **Audience:** Active prospects ready to hire Lawgical Group for transactional or dispute advice.
* **Key Content:**
  * **Segmented Inquire Form:** Includes drop-down selectors to capture inquiry types (e.g., Corporate Setup, Arbitration, Litigation, Debt Collection).
  * **Direct Coordinates:** Includes maps, direct corporate phone lines, and email.
* **Major Interactions:**
  * **Dynamic URL Prefilling:** Prefills form fields when visitors click "Discuss this Brief" from individual Insights pages, creating a personalized experience.
* **Business Value:** The core conversion point. Capturing targeted information allows the firm to respond with highly specific, pre-qualified strategy suggestions.
