# Future Roadmap
## Website Evolution & Scale Phases

This document maps out the proposed future updates for the Lawgical Group website, divided into three phases, along with their estimated impact on the business.

---

## Phase 1: Operational Autonomy & Search
*Focus: Adding content management capability and site search.*

### 1. Headless Content Management System (CMS) Integration
* **Description:** Connect a headless CMS (like Sanity, Contentful, or Strapy) to the website.
* **Why:** Allows administrative staff to publish legal insights and update partner profiles using an intuitive dashboard, without editing code files directly.
* **Impact:** **High.** Gives the marketing team complete operational independence.

### 2. Global Search System
* **Description:** Add a search bar inside the navbar to allow visitors to search services, industries, and insights.
* **Why:** Helps visitors quickly find legal briefs or specific practice areas.
* **Impact:** **Medium.** Enhances user navigation for returning visitors.

---

## Phase 2: Regional Customization & Engagement
*Focus: Deepening regional audience capture.*

### 3. Multilingual Support (Arabic Version)
* **Description:** Add an Arabic translation toggle that mirrors the layout (Right-to-Left styling) and displays translated content.
* **Why:** Crucial for local government clients, GCC advocates, and government entities in Riyadh and Abu Dhabi.
* **Impact:** **High.** Opens up direct instructions from local companies and Arabic-speaking partners.

### 4. Interactive Case Study System
* **Description:** Expand Client Testimonials into detailed, anonymous case studies detailing specific arbitration and corporate structuring campaigns.
* **Why:** Proves technical capability without disclosing client names.
* **Impact:** **Medium.** Increases trust signals for corporate clients.

---

## Phase 3: Client Portals & AI Integrations
*Focus: Turning the website into an active service platform.*

### 5. Secure Client Portal
* **Description:** Integrate a secure client login portal (like Clio, MyCase, or a custom portal).
* **Why:** Allows corporate clients to check case progress, view invoices, and share documents securely.
* **Impact:** **High.** Decreases administrative email volume and improves client satisfaction.

### 6. AI Legal assistant (Pre-qualification bot)
* **Description:** Deploy a custom AI assistant that answers general legal FAQs about GCC laws and directs visitors to relevant services or forms.
* **Why:** Pre-qualifies inquiries before they reach the advocates.
* **Impact:** **Medium.** Captures leads outside of standard working hours.

### 7. CRM Integration (HubSpot / Salesforce)
* **Description:** Connect contact forms directly to the firm's client relationship management (CRM) database.
* **Why:** Automates client onboarding, tracks follow-up times, and measures marketing returns.
* **Impact:** **High.** Maximizes sales operations efficiency.
