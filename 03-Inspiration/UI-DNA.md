# UI DNA

Goal: Do NOT look like a law firm website.

Positioning:
- Premium digital experience
- Editorial + technology + intelligence platform
- Less corporate, more immersive

Core references:
- UIVerse: interaction density
- ReactBits: motion components
- Supahero: hero experimentation
- Rebrand Gallery: typography
- CTA Gallery: conversion mechanics

Rules:
- Every section needs one signature interaction.
- No generic cards.
- No stock gradients.
- No template layouts.
